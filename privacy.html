Crisp · Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Crisp collects nothing. There is nothing to collect.

Crisp is a free, fully offline document scanner. It is built so that none of your documents or information ever leave your device.

Data we collect

None. Crisp does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or usage data. We have no servers and no analytics. We never see anything you scan.

Internet

Crisp has no internet permission and works fully in airplane mode. Your scans, documents, and recognised text are never uploaded anywhere.

Accounts

Crisp has no accounts, no sign-up, and no login. We do not know who you are.

Camera

Crisp uses the camera only to capture the pages you choose to scan. The camera is never used in the background, and images are processed entirely on your device.

Documents & files

When you import a PDF or pick an image, Crisp reads only the file you explicitly choose. Scans you save are written to Crisp's own private storage on your device. Nothing is shared until you tap Share and choose where it goes — Crisp never sends a document on its own.

Text recognition

Text recognition (OCR) runs fully on your device using a recognition model bundled inside the app. The text Crisp reads from your pages stays on your device and is never sent anywhere.

Advertising & tracking

Crisp contains no ads, no trackers, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no advertising identifiers.

Children

Crisp is a general-purpose utility and is safe for all audiences. Because it collects no data, it collects no data from children either.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
