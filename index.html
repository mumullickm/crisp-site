Crisp

Crisp turns your camera into a clean document scanner — free, offline, no watermark, no account, no ads, no tracking. It gives you the document, not the bill.

Help

  • Does it work offline? Yes. Crisp has no internet permission and works fully in airplane mode. Nothing you scan leaves your phone.
  • Can I select the text in my scan? Yes. Crisp adds an on-device text layer behind the image, so the PDF is searchable and you can copy the text — no subscription, no upload.
  • Where do my scans go? Into Crisp's offline library on your device. They stay there until you delete them, and they are only shared when you tap Share and choose where.
  • Why does re-editing soften a scan? Each edit re-renders the page from the saved PDF, so heavy re-editing loses a little sharpness. For the crispest result, re-scan the original.
